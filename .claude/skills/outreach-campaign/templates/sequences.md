# LinkedIn Outreach Sequence Templates

Pre-built templates for common outreach scenarios. Customize the bracketed sections for your business.

---

## Template 1: Warm Connect (2-step)

**Use when:** Connecting with people who already know of you (content engagers, mutual connections).

**Sequence:** Connect → DM (2 day wait)

**Connection Note (max 300 chars):**
```
Hey {{first_name}} — saw we share some connections in the [industry] space. Would love to connect and exchange notes on [topic]. No pitch, just genuine interest.
```

**DM 1:**
```
Hey {{first_name}}, thanks for connecting!

Quick context — I'm [your role/what you do]. Noticed you're working on [something relevant at {{company}}].

Curious: [specific question related to their work or a challenge they might face].

Either way, glad to be connected.
```

---

## Template 2: Cold Outreach (3-step)

**Use when:** Reaching out to prospects who don't know you.

**Sequence:** Connect → DM1 (2 day wait) → DM2 (3 day wait)

**Connection Note (max 300 chars):**
```
Hi {{first_name}} — I work with [type of companies] on [problem you solve]. Your work at {{company}} caught my eye. Would love to connect.
```

**DM 1:**
```
Hi {{first_name}}, thanks for accepting!

I'll keep it short — I help [ICP description] with [specific outcome].

Noticed {{company}} is [observation about their situation/challenge].

Would it make sense to chat for 15 min about [specific value prop]? No pressure either way.
```

**DM 2 (follow-up):**
```
Hey {{first_name}}, just bumping this up in case it got buried.

If the timing isn't right, totally understand — happy to reconnect down the road.

If it is, here's my calendar: [link]
```

## Template 3: Value-First (3-step)

**Use when:** You have a relevant resource, insight, or case study to share.

**Sequence:** Connect → DM1 (1 day wait) → DM2 (4 day wait)

**Connection Note (max 300 chars):**
```
Hi {{first_name}} — I put together a [resource type] on [topic relevant to their role]. Thought it might be useful for your team at {{company}}. Happy to share if you're interested.
```

**DM 1:**
```
Hey {{first_name}}, as promised — here's the [resource]:

[Link or brief summary]

Key takeaway: [one sentence insight relevant to them].

Would love to hear how this compares to what you're seeing at {{company}}.
```

**DM 2:**
```
Hi {{first_name}}, did you get a chance to look at [resource]?

Curious if [specific question about their situation].

Happy to share what we've learned working with [similar companies] if helpful.
```

---

## Customization Guide

When building a custom sequence:

1. **Connection note:** Max 300 characters. Include one personal detail — don't feel mass-sent.
2. **DM 1:** 3-5 sentences max. One clear CTA. Reference something specific about them.
3. **DM 2:** Even shorter. Acknowledge they're busy. Lower-commitment CTA.
4. **Wait times:**
   - After acceptance: 1-3 days (not same day — feels automated)
   - After DM 1: 3-5 days (give them time)
   - Never send on weekends
