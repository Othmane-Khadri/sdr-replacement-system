# Workflow: Signal-Based Outreach

Monitor buying signals and reach out when the timing is right.

## What Are Buying Signals?

Events that indicate a company might need your product:
- **Fundraise:** Just raised a round → likely hiring and buying tools
- **Hiring GTM roles:** Posting SDR/AE/RevOps jobs → scaling outbound
- **Product launch:** New product → new go-to-market needs
- **Leadership change:** New VP Sales → reviewing the stack

## Step 1: Find Signal-Rich Companies

```
You: "Find companies that recently raised Series A or B in the SaaS space"
```

Or use the lead qualifier with signals enabled:

```
You: "Qualify these leads with signals"
```

Gate 5.5 will web-search each company for:
- Recent funding rounds
- GTM hiring activity
- Product launches or partnerships

## Step 2: Craft Signal-Based Messages

Use the Value-First template and reference the signal:

```
Connection Note:
"Hi {{first_name}} — congrats on the [Series A/new hire/launch] at {{company}}.
I help [ICP] with [outcome] — would love to connect."

DM 1:
"Hey {{first_name}}, saw {{company}} just [signal]. That usually means [challenge they'll face].

We've helped [similar companies] navigate that — [specific result].

Worth a quick chat? No pressure."
```

## Step 3: Launch Campaign

```
You: "Create outreach campaign for signal-matched leads"
```

Signal-based campaigns typically see 2-3x higher accept rates than cold outreach because the timing is relevant.

## Refresh Cycle

Run this monthly:
1. Search for new companies matching your signal criteria
2. Qualify with signals enabled
3. Launch targeted campaigns
4. Track results
