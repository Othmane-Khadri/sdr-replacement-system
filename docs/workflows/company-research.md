# Workflow: Company Research

Find and enrich companies matching your target criteria, then find decision makers.

## Step 1: Search for Companies

```
You: "Find B2B SaaS companies in the US with 50-200 employees in the sales tech space"
```

Crustdata returns matching companies with: name, website, industry, employee count, location, funding.

## Step 2: Enrich Top Picks

```
You: "Enrich acme.com, betacorp.io, and gammatech.com"
```

Gets detailed profiles: full funding history, web traffic, LinkedIn followers, etc.

## Step 3: Find Decision Makers

```
You: "Find VPs of Sales and Founders at these companies"
```

Crustdata people search returns profiles with: name, title, company, LinkedIn URL, seniority.

## Step 4: Qualify and Outreach

Feed the people results into the qualification pipeline:

```
You: "Qualify these leads"
```

Then launch a campaign:

```
You: "Create outreach campaign for hot leads"
```

## Cost Estimate

| Action | Credits | Approx Cost |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| Company search (1 query) | 1 | ~$0.02 |
| Enrich 10 companies | 10-40 | ~$0.50 |
| People search (50 results) | 3 | ~$0.06 |
| **Total** | ~15-45 | **~$0.60** |
