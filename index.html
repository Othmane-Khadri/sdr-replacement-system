The Skills

5 Claude Code skills. Ready to use.

Just open Claude Code in the repo and talk naturally. Skills trigger automatically.

SKILL 01

LinkedIn Engagement Scraper

Scrape commenters and likers from any LinkedIn post URL. Auto-paginates, exports JSON + CSV. Your first lead source.

SKILL 02

Find People & Companies

Search companies by industry, size, location. Find decision makers by title and seniority. Your second lead source.

Bulk email and phone enrichment. Async processing with smart polling. Outputs verified contact data.
